Our Story

20+ Years In The Industry.
10 Running Our Own.

SL Installations was founded on one principle: install every piece of furniture as if it were going into our own space. That standard has not changed since day one.

With over two decades of hands on experience, we understand what real projects look like. Tight timelines, last minute floor plan changes, and the pressure of keeping a business running through a transition.

We bring 20 trained installers, our own fleet of vehicles, and a commitment to clean sites and fast turnarounds. We handle:

  • Full office build-outs
  • Furniture reconfigurations
  • Weekend and after hours installs
  • Decommissions and relocations
