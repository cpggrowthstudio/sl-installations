Before you start — what you need
✅ Google account — use the business Gmail (slochan@slinstallations.ca or create one at gmail.com)
✅ Business address or service area — SL can list as a service area business with no physical address shown
✅ Phone number — 647-504-3169
✅ Photos — at least 5 project photos and the logo
1

Go to Google Business Profile

Visit business.google.com and click Manage now. Sign in with the business Google account.

2

Enter the business name

Type SL Installations & Solutions Inc. — if Google suggests it already exists, select it. Otherwise click Add your business to Google.

3

Choose the business category

Search for and select Commercial Furniture Installer. This is the most important field for showing up in local search.

Tip: You can add secondary categories later — "Office Furniture Store" or "Moving Company" can help capture extra searches.
4

Set up as a service area business

When asked if you have a location customers visit, select No. Then add your service areas — start with Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Oakville, and Ontario.

5

Add phone number and website

Phone: 647-504-3169. Website: slinstallations.ca (once live — use the demo link for now).

6

Verify the business

Google will ask you to verify. Choose phone call or text to 647-504-3169 — it's the fastest option. Enter the code when it arrives.

Note: Some accounts get postcard verification by mail which takes 1–2 weeks. If that happens, you can still complete the profile now and verify when it arrives.
7

Complete the profile

Once verified, go to Info and fill in the business description, services, and hours using the copy from the Ready to Use Copy tab.

8

Add photos

Go to Photos and upload: the logo, a cover photo, and at least 5 project photos. More photos = higher ranking. Aim for 15–20 to start.

Tip: Add the same photos from the website gallery. Label them well — Google reads file names. E.g. toronto-office-furniture-installation.jpg
9

Ask for the first reviews

Go to Get more reviews and copy the direct review link. Send it to Shane and Kevin's top 5 happy clients right away. Reviews are the fastest way to rank higher.

Already have 12 Google reviews. Make sure they're all showing on the new profile — they may need to be claimed or they'll carry over automatically once the profile is verified.
